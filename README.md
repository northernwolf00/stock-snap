# StockSnap

**Offline Inventory Management System for Small Businesses**

StockSnap is a simple and powerful offline inventory management solution designed for small shops, warehouses, and pop-up businesses. Its core strength is its ability to operate fully without an internet connection, making it ideal for locations with unreliable connectivity.

## 🚀 Key Features

- **📷 Barcode Scanner:** Use your phone's camera to scan product barcodes accurately and quickly, reducing manual data entry.
- **📦 Offline Functionality:** Manage your entire inventory without an internet connection. All data is stored locally on your device.
- **🔔 Low Stock Alerts:** Get automatic notifications when product levels fall below a specific threshold (no internet required).
- **🏬 Multi-Location Support:** Manage multiple shops or warehouses from a single system. Keep track of stock levels across different sites.
- **🔄 Automatic Synchronization:** When an internet connection becomes available, your data automatically syncs across devices and back up to the cloud.
- **👥 Pro Version (Multi-User):** Collaborate with your team by using StockSnap on multiple devices simultaneously with the Pro version.
- **⚡ Easy to Use:** A clean and intuitive interface that anyone can learn in under 5 minutes. No technical expertise required.

## 💼 Who is StockSnap for?

StockSnap is specifically tailored for:
- **Small Shop Owners** (Grocery, clothing, electronics, etc.)
- **Warehouse Managers**
- **Market Vendors** (Bazaar sellers)
- **Mobile Sales Teams** (Pop-up shops, delivery services)

## 🎯 Main Benefits

- **Save Time:** Automate stock counting and data entry.
- **Reduce Errors:** Minimize human mistakes with barcode scanning.
- **Stay Organized:** Maintain a clear and structured overview of all your products.
- **Reliable Anywhere:** Work anywhere, anytime, regardless of internet availability.
